NYC and Toronto-based actress and model, Olivia Jackson brings a rare versatility to every role — moving effortlessly between vulnerability, wit, and quiet strength. With a playing age of 16–23, she inhabits the full emotional spectrum of young adult experience.

Pursuing an Honours B.Sc. in Chemistry at the University of Toronto — with minors in Biology and Mathematics — Olivia brings analytical discipline and intellectual precision to her craft. The rigor of science and the instinct of performance are two sides of the same coin.

Her triple citizenship and command of American, Canadian, and British accents make her a uniquely flexible collaborator for productions on either side of the Atlantic — and everywhere in between.
